About 

Gallery Exchange is an antique goods business founded in 1991. We began buying and selling furniture in the North-Central Illinois area operating out of our garage, and eventually moving into an old farm property to set up our store. 

 We have had every kind of collectible come through our shop, from original Salvador Dali's to 16th century royal sofas. Collectors flock to our store year round for pieces from many ages and places around the world.
